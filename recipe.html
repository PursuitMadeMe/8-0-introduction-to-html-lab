Turkey & Cheese Sandwich

Description

Ham and American Cheese is the perfect school trip lunch!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white bread
  • 5 pieces of turkey
  • 1 slice American Cheese
  • Head of lettuce
  • Tamato
  • Mayo
  • Sharp Knife
  • Butter Knife
  • Cutting board

Directions

  1. Lay both slices of bread flat on the cutting board
  2. Use the butter knife to spread Mayo on both sides of the bread
  3. Place the American cheese on one side of the bread
  4. Place the 5 slices of turkey on top of the cheese
  5. Add 2 leaves of lettuce
  6. Use sharp knife to cut tomato slices
  7. Add 2 slices of tomato
  8. Place the other slice of bread ontop of the turkey
  9. Cut in half with sharp kniife
  10. Enjoy
